Supermarkets

  • Marsmanplein

    • Albert Heijn

      Albert Heijn is a supermarket that can easily be noticed by its blue color and logo. This supermarket can be found on the right side of the small square that is surrounded by Marsmanplein.

    • Plus

      The Plus is a supermarket that primarily uses a green color, although its logo consists of many more. This supermarket can be found on the inside of Marsmanplein on the right side.

  • Eksterlaan

    • Dekamarkt

      The Dekamarkt is further away from Marsmanplein and uses a red square logo. It can be found on the Eksterlaan next to a fitness school.
